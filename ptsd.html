post traumatic stress disorder (ptsd)

Post traumatic stress disorder is a disorder in which a person has difficulty recovering after experiencing or witnessing a terrifying event. People who have PTSD may feel stressed or frightened, even when they are not in danger. Symptoms usually begin early, within 3 months of the traumatic incident, but sometimes they begin years afterward. Anyone can develop PTSD at any age.

additional symptoms include:

  • flashbacks
  • frightening thoughts
  • bad dreams
  • being easily startled
  • feeling tense or on edge

these symptoms must last for a month to be considered ptsd
